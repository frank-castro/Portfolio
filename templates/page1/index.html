Welcome to Frank’s personal website.

Frank is an experienced designer and Creative Director living in Ottawa, Canada. He has designed many things in the past but nowadays he focuses on web design.

He believes it is important to code his own designs so he creates websites and prototypes using AngularJS. Sometimes he thinks of himself as a programmer but he is just a designer who likes to code.

In a few words, Frank builds websites. In more specific terms, he defines the quality of the user experience, the art direction, design the visual elements, and implement / manage the front end development of web applications for different platforms and devices.

He is currently employed at Phirelight as Creative Director responsible for the user experience design and prototyping of RapidPhire; the company’s software solution for network security.
